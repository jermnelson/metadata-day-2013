Introducing BIBFRAME

BIBFRAME -- for Bibliographic Framework -- is an effort by the Library of Congress to replace MARC21 and MARCXML with a new Linked Data-based vocabulary for bibliographic description.

Creative Work

A resource reflecting a conceptual essence of the cataloging resource. (source: bibframe.org)

BIBFRAME provides specific types of Creative Works, such as some examples:

Serial MusicalAudio SoftwareOrMultimedia

Instance

Authority

A resource reflecting key authority concepts that have defined relationships reflected in the Work and Instance. (source: bibframe.org)

Annotation

A resource that adds additional information to or about other BIBFRAME resources when knowing 'who' annotated it is important. (source: bibframe.org)
